ingredients: |-
  * 1 tsp turmeric powder
  * 3/4 tsp ginger powder
  * 3/4 tsp ground pepper
  * 3/4 tsp cinnamon or 1 cinnamon stick
  * Slice of lemon
steps: |-
  * Mix dry ingredients and add to a cup
  * Add boiling water and mix
  * Add slice of lemon if you wish
  * Add honey to taste
